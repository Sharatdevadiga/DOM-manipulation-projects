Palindrome checker

💡A palindrome is a word or phrase that can be read the same way forwards and backwards, ignoring punctuation, case, and spacing.

    For example :
  • ✅ radar
  • ✅ A man, a plan, a canal, Panama!
  • ✅ 12321
  • .
  • ❌ radars
  • ❌ A woman, a plan, a canal, Panama!
  • ❌ 12345
